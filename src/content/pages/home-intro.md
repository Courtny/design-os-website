# Design Operating System

Your AI-powered copilot for product design. Built for Claude Code and Cursor.

Most designers use AI for one-off questions with zero context about their actual design system, past decisions, or research. Design OS works differently: it organizes your design knowledge so every output reflects how your team actually works.
